Willsthoughts is an evolving notebook of technology, culture, and personal observations. It is written from Kingston, Jamaica with an eye for the global pulse—highlighting voices, tools, and trends that deserve a closer listen.

On any given week you might find deep dives into developer workflows, sonic journeys through dancehall history, or notes from current experiments in open source. The goal is to spark curiosity, encourage thoughtful debate, and keep a record of the lessons learned along the way.

Have an idea or collaboration in mind? Reach out via hello@willsthoughts.com— thoughtful conversations are always welcome.
