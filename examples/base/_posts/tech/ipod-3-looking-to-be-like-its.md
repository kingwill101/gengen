According to a post on [tapscape.com](http://tapscape.com/)  the iPad 3 looks set to launch at the same price as the iPad 2 according to a [tip-off](http://9to5mac.com/2012/03/02/ipad-3-prices-and-likely-features-revealed/) published by _9to5Mac_. Accorging to them there post not only will the iPad 3 cost the same as the iPad 2, but it will also be available in the same three sizes as its predecessor – 16GB, 32GB and 64GB.
